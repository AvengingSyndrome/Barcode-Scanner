The Premise

How often do you see a book in a store or a library and think "I'm totally going to look that up later" and promptly forget about it? This is for those moments.

The book scanner app lets you enter an ISBN, or scan a barcode, and adds it to a list of saved books, sending you notifications at a time of your choice.

Languages

The Book Scanner app is written with the Android Java SDK for the backend, and in boostrap-flavoured html/css/javascript for the frontend.
